About GHISA

Who We Are

GHISA is a student association dedicated to academic excellence, leadership development, and community engagement. The Gujba Higher Institution Students Association (GHISA) is a community-based organization uniting students from Gujba Local Government Area in Yobe State who are pursuing higher education across Nigeria. It focuses on fostering academic excellence, unity, career guidance, and community development, with key leaders including President Abubakar Mohammed Inuwa. Facebook Facebook +1 Key details about the association include: Mission: To support students from Gujba attending various institutions—such as Yobe State University and Shehu Sule College of Nursing—through mentorship, scholarships, and educational resources. Activities: Organizes JAMB training programs, career guidance workshops, and manages scholarship opportunities, often supported by local patrons. Leadership/Structure: The group is supported by patrons like Ibrahim Haruna and Malam Hassan Shetima. The National Executive Council (NEC) manages affairs, with recent election processes handled by a committee including Chairman Bunu Kachallah and Secretary General El-Abdul Sha'tan Arab
