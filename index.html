GHISA (Gujba Higher Institution Students Association) is an organization that brings together students from Gujba Local Government who are studying in different higher institutions. The association aims to promote unity, academic excellence, leadership development, and community service among its members.

Through educational support programs, students development initiatives, and community outreach activities, GHISA continues to inspire young students to pursue their academic goals and contribute positively to the development of society.

Gujba Higher Institution Students Association Programs

GHISA (Gujba Higher Institution Students Association) is a students-led organization committed to promoting academic excellence, leadership development, and community service within Gujba Local Government Area. Ghisa Jamb program in Buni yadi

GHISA Support for Education Development

One of the most important programs organized by GHISA is the support and orientation program for students preparing for the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) examination. Many secondary school students often face challenges in understanding the admission process and preparing effectively for their examinations. GHISA organizes guidance sessions where experienced students from universities and other higher institutions share their knowledge and experiences. During these sessions, students are educated on how to properly register for JAMB, how to choose suitable courses and institutions, and how to prepare academically for the examination. This program also motivates students to aim for higher education and helps them understand the opportunities available to them after completing secondary school.
